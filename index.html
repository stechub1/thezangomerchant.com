About Us

We are a trusted agency providing a wide range of merchant services. At D.Z.M.A.A., we believe that honesty and reliability are the foundations of business. With our extensive network and experience, we help connect buyers and sellers, source products, and now offer digital KYC services through Sidra.

D. ZANGO MERCHANT AND AGENCIES is a proudly Nigerian company based in Kaduna, offering reliable general merchant services and Sidra KYC onboarding. We stand by one core principle: Honesty is the Best Policy.

We work with individuals, SMEs, and businesses across Nigeria to provide import/export assistance, documentation, business consulting, and identity verification services—all with integrity and speed.

Our professional team is committed to helping you succeed with trusted solutions and real human service.

Our Journey

  • 2020: D.Z.M.A.A. was founded to help merchants grow with honest and reliable services.
  • 2023: Expanded to offer digital documentation and client support nationwide.
  • 2025: Applied to become an official Sidra KYC onboarding partner to help more Nigerians verify securely.
